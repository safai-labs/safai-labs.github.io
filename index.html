30 years of inevitability.

  1. 1994
    Remedium Security Systems — first kernel-level Unix/Linux daemon self-defense system (Exorcist). The origin of the self-defending data concept.
  2. 2009
    CWID 2009 Blue Ribbon — DoD Red Team's first-ever failed breach attempt in a cross-domain scenario. SINET 2010 Innovator Award — National Press Club, Washington DC.
  3. 2018
    saf.ai, Inc. founded — Resiliate development begins. Cybernetic Engrams® concept formalized. Named in honour of Safdar Masud.
  4. 2019
    Patent priority date — US provisional filed January 21, 2019. International filing strategy initiated.
  5. 2022
    F5 Networks production deployment — Resiliate Cybernetic Engrams® deployed under NDA for real-time anomaly detection processing ~1 billion HTTP requests per hour.
  6. 2024
    US Patent 12,045,224 granted — international filings active (CA, EP). Active acquisition discussions underway.
